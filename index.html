About Me

Mr. Sabir Mujawar is an accomplished academician, researcher, and finance expert with a strong foundation in both Finance and Information Technology. With over 9 years of professional experience across academia and industry, he brings a unique blend of theoretical depth and practical insight to his work. Currently serving as an Assistant Professor at Maratha Mandir’s Babasaheb Gawde Institute of Management Studies, he is known for his engaging teaching style, innovative curriculum design, and student-centric mentorship. Sabir currently pursuing Ph.D. in Business Economics, an MBA in Finance, and is MH-SET certified in Commerce. His academic pursuits are complemented by his passion for research, with multiple peer-reviewed publications, UGC-CARE-listed articles, and book contributions in areas like taxation, banking, digital transformation, and investment behavior. He has also authored and edited university textbooks, underscoring his dedication to knowledge dissemination. With prior roles at reputed institutions like Chetana’s College and corporate giants like JPMorgan Chase, Sabir has cultivated a dynamic approach to leadership, technology integration, and academic excellence. He has successfully led student development programs, organized national-level conferences, and played a pivotal role in accreditation processes (NAAC/NBA). A creative thinker and skilled communicator, Sabir is also a certified digital marketing and analytics professional with expertise in tools like Power BI, CorelDRAW, and Photoshop. Beyond academics, he is a recognized contributor to social service and a committed promoter of student-led research. Driven by purpose, powered by learning, and guided by values—Sabir Mujawar continues to make a meaningful impact in the world of education, research, and finance. Connect with me on Google Scholar.
